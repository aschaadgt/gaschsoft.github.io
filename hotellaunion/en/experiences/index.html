Questions that
actually help.

Do rates include admission and meals?

It depends on the option. Before you confirm, we explain exactly what each experience includes and what you will need to pay separately.

Can I book if I am not staying at the hotel?

Message us with your date and the service you want. We will check availability and the most convenient meeting point.

Do tours depart every day?

Most run regularly, but schedules and availability change. We confirm everything with you before any payment.

What happens if the weather changes?

Safety comes first. If an operator changes or cancels a departure, we will help review the available alternatives.
