Growing since 1987

La Unión started small.
The view was always immense.

Before becoming a hotel, La Unión was a shop on the street that gave it its name. Then came a restaurant, a second floor, a third, and a family determined to keep improving the place without erasing its character.

Today the house looks onto Lake Petén Itzá and welcomes travelers who arrive with a route in mind—or discover it while they are here.
